Who We Are

Jai Mata Trading has been a leader in the agricultural market for over a decade, specializing in grains such as wheat and maize. Our facilities include a state-of-the-art 100T Dharam Kanta for accurate and efficient vehicle weighing.

Jai Mata Trading is your one-stop shop for all your grain, weighing, and produce needs. We are a dedicated firm offering:

  • Grain Trading: We buy and sell a variety of grains, including wheat, maize, and others, at competitive prices.
  • Weighbridge Services: Our reliable 100-ton dharam kanta ensures accurate weighing for all types of vehicles.
  • Wholesale Fruits & Vegetables: We are a trusted supplier of fresh and high-quality fruits and vegetables at wholesale prices.
